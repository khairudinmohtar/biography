INTEREST

RUNNING

Running is more than just a hobby for me, it’s a vital part of my lifestyle. It allows me to clear my mind, stay fit, and challenge myself both physically and mentally. Running is part of maintaining my productivity and focus at work as well. Running has improved my endurance, boosted my energy levels, and taught me discipline.

HIKING

Hiking is one of my favorite ways to disconnect from daily stress and reconnect with nature. Whether it's a local trail or a mountain path, each hike offers a chance to breathe fresh air, enjoy scenic views, and reset mentally. The physical challenge of hiking improves my stamina, while the quiet surroundings give me space to think clearly. These benefits often carry over into my work life—after a good hike, I return more focused, creative, and motivated.
